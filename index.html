Cat Scientist · Est. 2024 · Fremont, CA

We started with a question,
and a cat who wouldn't sit still.

At Cat Scientist, we believe that thoughtful design makes all the difference — for both you and your feline friends. Our handcrafted products are made to elevate the everyday moments of your cat's life, blending comfort, style, and a bit of science to create pieces that cats love and humans appreciate.

Through a series of playful "fabric experiments" with our own cats, we discovered what they truly gravitated toward — the textures, the give, the warmth, the shape. Every insight from those sessions went into what became the Cat Cloud: a bean bag-style bed engineered for feline happiness.

We're a small, independent studio out of Fremont, California, sewing each piece by hand and shipping it with care. We're not chasing mass production. We're chasing that moment when your cat finds their new favorite spot — and stays.

i.

Designed with Curiosity

Every product begins with observation — watching how cats move, rest, stretch, and nest. We let feline behavior guide every design decision.

ii.

Crafted by Hand

Each piece is sewn with care, not manufactured at scale. You're getting something made with intention — a small-batch object built to last and to be loved.

iii.

Home-Worthy Aesthetic

Your cat's things should be as beautiful as yours. We design for the human home too — soft palettes, clean lines, prints that complement real interiors.

The Cat Cloud

A bed worthy of
their noblest naps.

Born from our fabric experiments, the Cat Cloud is a bean bag-style bed engineered with the perfect balance of softness and structure. It holds its shape so your cat always has a defined, cozy haven — not just a flat pillow that collapses under them.

Finished in soft, home-friendly prints that blend into your space rather than fighting it. Each one is handcrafted, so yours is one of a kind.
